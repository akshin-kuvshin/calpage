CALPAGE short docs:

'-v' or '--version'
    - prints information about the current version of the program and its author.

'-h' or '--help'
    - prints this text.

'ru' or 'en'
    - sets the style of calendar page on the one you entered.

'today'
    - prints the calendar page of current month.

'MM YYYY' or 'YYYY MM'
    - prints the calendar page of entered month in entered year.
